## Data of an Unusual Size (2024 edition): A practical guide to analysis and interactive visualization of massive datasets

Tutorial on the fundamentals of big data computation from a practical usage lens. It covers distributed computing with Dask, basics of Polars and DuckDB, and interactive visualization+dashboards with hvPlot+Panel, while working on the cloud with real ~70GB of big data.

* [**SciPy US 2024 (upcoming) · 9th July**](https://cfp.scipy.org/2024/talk/UKLNLQ/)
